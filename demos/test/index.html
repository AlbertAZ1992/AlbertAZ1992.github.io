ABOUT @ JIA SHI
I am a visual designer in San Francisco. I hold a Master of Arts degree from the Academy of Art University in the area of Web Design and New Media. I am passionate about great UX/UI and graphic design. I am currently available for professional design services. I have skills in Adobe design applications such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, and Premiere. I also have good working knowledge of front end web technologies. I plan to build a career working in collaborative corporate environments, and I am seeking an entry-level internship position. Please feel free to talk to me and let's do creative design together.
